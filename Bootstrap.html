What do you want to know about Frontend ?

If you want to learn about a frontend developer, you should ask about their core technical skills like proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, their understanding of responsive design, experience with JavaScript frameworks (React, Angular, Vue), knowledge of version control systems like Git, their ability to work with design mockups, their approach to cross-browser compatibility, and how they prioritize user experience when building website interfaces.
